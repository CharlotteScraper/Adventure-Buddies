# Adventure Buddies — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 20, 2026**

## Our Commitment to Privacy
Adventure Buddies is designed for children ages 2–5. We take your family's privacy seriously and are fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## Information We Collect
**We collect NO personal information.**
- The app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII)
- The app does NOT use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
- The app does NOT require an account, login, or email address
- The child's name entered during onboarding is stored **only locally** on the device and is never sent to any server

## Information We Do NOT Collect
- No names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses
- No device identifiers or advertising IDs
- No location data
- No browsing history or usage data
- No photos, audio recordings, or videos
- No social media accounts or connections

## Local Data Storage
All data (child name, buddy customization, game progress, settings) is stored **exclusively on-device** using SQLite and SharedPreferences. No data ever leaves your device.

## Parent Dashboard
The Parent Dashboard shows learning progress stats. This information never leaves the device.

## Third-Party Services
This app uses:
- **sqflite** — Local database (all data stays on-device)
- **shared_preferences** — Local settings storage (all data stays on-device)
- **audioplayers** — Local audio playback (no network access)
- **path_provider** — Local file system access (no network access)

None of these services transmit data off-device.

## No In-App Purchases or Ads
Adventure Buddies is a premium paid app with no subscriptions, no in-app purchases, and no advertisements. There is no mechanism for a child to accidentally make purchases.

## Changes to This Policy
If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the app and notify parents through the App Store update description.

## Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the App Store support channel.
